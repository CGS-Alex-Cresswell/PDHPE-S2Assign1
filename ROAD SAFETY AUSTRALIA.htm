Help Make The Roads A Safer Place

Dont use your phone while driving

The road is a dangerous environment, using you phone only makes it worse. During 2016 13% of death on Queensland road were caused by mobile phone usage while driving.

Driver distraction is one of the main causes of crashes on Australian roads. Over the years cell phone usage has become an essential part of life and this has meant that people using their phones while driving has become a detrimental risk on peoples lives.

When you use your phone while you drive you are not just putting yourself in extreme danger you are putting other in danger too. When you are driving through school zones dont pick up the phone pay attention!

According to the ‘Queensland Centre for Accident Research & Road Safety’ when you use your phone while driving the risk of having an accident increases four fold.

This isn’t a small issue, the ‘The Sydney Morning Herald’ reported that around 42% of all Australian have reported using mobile phones while driving.

 

PUT IT DOWN

Dont pick up your phone while driving!

At 55 mph For every 5 seconds you are on your phone you have travelled the equivalent of a football field BLIND.

 

Take Action

Take steps to be hands free, set up your playlist before driving. Set up your GPS before you go.

 

What counts?

- Sending or receiving a text message

- Using your phones map system

- Changing the song on your phone

- Reading content on a phone screen

- Interacting with a phone’s apps

- Making or receiving calls, this includes using handsfree.
